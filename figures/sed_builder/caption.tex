\textit{\textbf{\label{fig:sed_builder} SED Builder}} An example view of the SED Builder. On the right side of the window is a list of SEDs open for analysis; in this case, \textit{FilterSed} is selected. The Segments or components that constitute \textit{FilterSed} are shown in the Segments section. Segments may be managed separately. Highlighed SEDs or Segments may be edited, removed, saved or broadcast to an external SAMP-enabled application. New Segments and SEDs are added to the Iris session through the SED Builder.
